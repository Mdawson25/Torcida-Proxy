Shapes & sizes
Cigars are measured by length (inches) and ring gauge (64ths of an inch). Shape affects draw speed, burn rate, and flavor development.
Strength guide
Strength refers to nicotine content and body — not flavor intensity. Always eat before smoking a full-strength cigar.

Types of cuts

How to light a cigar
Lighting is a ritual. Done properly it ensures an even, flavorful smoke from first draw to the final third. Never rush it.

What is Torcida

Torcida is your AI-powered cigar companion — built for everyone from the first-time smoker to the seasoned collector. Search any cigar for rich tasting notes and pairing recommendations, track your humidor, log every smoke, and explore the craft through a beautifully designed reference guide.

One app. Every part of the ritual.

Cigar Culture is for Everyone

You don't need twenty years of experience to deserve a seat at the table. All you need is curiosity and a willingness to slow down. Torcida was built to make the cigar world as welcoming to the newcomer as it is rewarding to the aficionado.

"In the cigar lounge, the corner office and the first job blur together. The smoke is the same. The pause is the same. The moment is the same."
— The Torcida Philosophy
Why We Are Called Torcida

Torcida is the feminine past participle of "to roll" in Spanish and Portuguese — a deliberate tribute to the women whose skilled hands have built the premium cigar industry for generations. Their names are rarely on the bands, but without them, there is no cigar. In Portuguese, torcida also means a passionate, devoted fan base — which is exactly what we are building.
